# Instagram Non-Followers Checker

This Chrome extension allows you to easily find out which of your Instagram followings are not following you back. 

## Features
- Scans your followers and following lists directly from your browser.
- Secure: No login required to a third-party app. All requests happen locally in your browser.
- Displays a clean list of users who do not follow you back, complete with profile pictures and links to their profiles.

## Installation Instructions (Chrome)

Since this extension is not published on the Chrome Web Store, you will need to install it manually using Chrome's Developer Mode.

1. **Download or Clone the Repository**
   Make sure you have all the files for this extension in a folder on your computer (e.g., `ig-non-followers-ext`).

2. **Open Chrome Extensions Page**
   - Open Google Chrome.
   - Click the three-dot menu in the top right corner.
   - Go to **Extensions** > **Manage Extensions**.
   - Alternatively, type `chrome://extensions/` in your address bar and press Enter.

3. **Enable Developer Mode**
   - In the top right corner of the Extensions page, toggle the switch for **Developer mode** to ON.

4. **Load the Extension**
   - Click the **Load unpacked** button that appears in the top left.
   - Browse your computer and select the folder containing the extension files (`ig-non-followers-ext`).
   - Click **Select Folder** (or Open).

5. **Pin the Extension (Optional but recommended)**
   - Click the puzzle piece icon next to your profile picture in Chrome's toolbar.
   - Find "Instagram Non-Followers" in the list and click the pin icon next to it for easy access.

## How to Use

1. **Log in to Instagram:** Open a new tab and go to [instagram.com](https://www.instagram.com). Make sure you are logged into your account.
2. **Open the Extension:** Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar.
3. **Start the Scan:** Click the "Check Non-Followers" button.
4. **Wait for Processing:** The extension will start scanning your followers and followings. Please do not close the Instagram tab while this is happening. The time it takes will depend on how many people you follow.
5. **View Results:** Once the scan is complete, a new tab will automatically open displaying the list of users who are not following you back. You can click on their profile pictures or usernames to visit their profiles.

## Troubleshooting

- **Extension doesn't do anything:** Make sure you are on `instagram.com` when you click the extension.
- **Results are empty/incorrect:** Instagram occasionally changes its website structure. If the extension stops working, the code may need to be updated to reflect those changes.
- **Rate limiting:** If you have a massive amount of followers/following, Instagram might temporarily rate-limit your requests. Try again later if the extension fails to fetch data.
